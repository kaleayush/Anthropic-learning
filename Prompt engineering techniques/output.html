**1. Daily Calorie and Macronutrient Targets:**

To determine the appropriate calorie intake, we first estimate the athlete's Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and then factor in their activity level.

*   **Assumptions:**
    *   Age: We'll assume the athlete is 25 years old (a common assumption when age isn't provided).
    *   Gender: We'll assume male, as caloric needs are typically higher than females for the same weight and activity level. If the athlete is female, caloric needs would be slightly lower.
    *   Activity Level: "Maintain muscle mass and endurance" implies a very active lifestyle, engaging in moderate to vigorous exercise 6-7 days per week. We will use an activity factor of 1.725.

*   **BMR Calculation (Mifflin-St Jeor Equation for males):**
    BMR = (10 * weight in kg) + (6.25 * height in cm) - (5 * age) + 5
    BMR = (10 * 70) + (6.25 * 175) - (5 * 25) + 5
    BMR = 700 + 1093.75 - 125 + 5 = 1673.75 kcal

*   **Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE):**
    TDEE = BMR * Activity Factor
    TDEE = 1673.75 kcal * 1.725 = 2886 kcal

    **Target Daily Calories: ~2900 kcal**

*   **Macronutrient Split (for muscle maintenance & endurance):**
    *   **Protein:** 1.9 g/kg body weight = 1.9 * 70 kg = 133 grams (approx. 532 kcal)
    *   **Fat:** 25% of total calories = 0.25 * 2900 kcal = 725 kcal / 9 kcal/g = 80.5 grams
